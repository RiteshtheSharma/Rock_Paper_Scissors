Rock, Paper & Scissors Game

Please read below instructions carefully to understand how to play the game

  • You need to press one of the 3 buttons i.e 'Rock', 'Paper' and 'Scissors'
  • When One of the 2 i.e you or program win 5 times then game will over and result will be displayed
  • All the sequence of events occur during the game will be displayed at the end of game
  • You can RestartBtn the game using 'RestartBtn' button which will be enabled at the end of game
